Ga Xot Cay

Korean-style spicy fried chicken is a delicious fried chicken dish with typical spicy taste of Gochujang chili, the chicken has a crispy crust, inside the chicken is tender and sweet.

Ingredients for Korean style spicy fried chicken: Chicken thigh 350 grams Crispy flour 350 grams Gochujang 1 tbsp (You can buy it at a Korean supermarket or convenience store) Sugar 50 grams Seasoning seeds 3 grams Ground pepper 3 grams Honey 130 ml Ground pepper 3 grams Soy sauce 3 tbsp Onions 1 branch paro Chili powder 3 grams Roasted white sesame seeds 5 grams

Process materials The chicken thighs are washed, filtered all the bones, just take the meat and cut pieces to taste. Marinate the chicken with 3 grams of ground pepper and 3 grams of seasoning for 15 minutes. The crispy flour is divided into two bowls, a 200-gram bowl and a 150-gram bowl. Part 150 gr powder you dissolve in 150 ml of water, stir to dissolve. Onions you take the stump, cut into pieces
