After 1975, according to Vietnamese migrants and crossing the sea, the bread appeared in many countries and gradually became popular all over the world. In a normal call, Americans call this dish instead of "Sandwich" as the way they call the same dishes. In March 2012, the tourist page of The Guardian - a famous Australian newspaper, voted Saigon bread in the top 10 delicious and attractive street dishes in the world.

Vietnamese bread (also known as bread) is a very popular Vietnamese food, including shells as a crispy leather toast, soft intestine; Inside is the multiplication. Depending on the domain taste or personal preference that people will form different types of multipliers (usually silk, meat, fish, vegetarian food or fruit jam ... accompanied by a number of side materials Other like Pats, Butter, Vegetables, Chili, Sour ...). This type of bread originated from the Baguette cake brought into South Vietnam from the previous centuries (someone said that this dish was available in Vietnam 150 years ago). Later, the range of bread effects spread throughout the Central and the South, especially a very popular bread in Saigon. In the process of improvement, Saigon people prepared the Baguette cake into a small and shorter bread drive, only about 30-40 cm, and the intestine was hollier to be able to put the multiplication into the middle of the two cake shells, Similar to clamps. [5] Depending on the multiplication component is clamped inside that bread has different names. In addition, people can also serve them with many different foods.
